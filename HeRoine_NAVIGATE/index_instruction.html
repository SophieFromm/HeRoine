Later you will see a helicopter. Bags with money will drop from the helicopter. Your task is to catch them.

Try to win as much money as possible.
The money you win will turn into real payment after the experiment.

This is the helicopter when the sky is clear.


The mouse moves the cursor as a bucket. You use the bucket to catch what the helicopter drops. Try to position the bucket directly under the helicopter.

Press the left mouse button to confirm. Then, the cursor will turn black and stop moving.
Give it a try and move forward to the next part of the instruction.


After you clicked, you see the distance between your bucket and its actual position.

Later you will only have 3 seconds to decide.

Sometimes the helicopter drops a money bag.
When you catch it you will receive money.

Other times, the helicopter drops a sand bag.
You will not get money for it.
Try to catch it anyway!

Where exactly the bag drops will vary. How much it varies will depend on whether it is more or less windy. Thus, the location of the bag depends on the location of the helicopter and how windy it is.

Usually, the helicopter stays in the same place.
Occasionally, it moves to a completely new location.
